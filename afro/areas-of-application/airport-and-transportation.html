Airport & Transportation

Maintaining secure access control for passengers and staff is challenging for airports and public transportation due to the growing number of travelers, leading to long lines and potential security risks. Implementing our facial recognition technology in airports and public transportation will enhance the overall experience for travelers while ensuring their safety and security.

Explore

Airport Access Control

Our solution:

Sentinel Access Control enables smooth and secure airport entry and boarding with our technology used at airport check-in and boarding gates. Sentinel compares a live image of a person's face with their presented identification document and grants access to restricted areas based on a successful match.  

Biometric Ticketing

Our solution:

With biometric ticketing, passengers can simply scan their face to confirm their identity and validate their ticket. This eliminates the need to physically present a ticket or use a contactless card, reducing wait times and speeding up the boarding process.
