Areas of application/

Sports & Entertainment

Access control is an important issue to consider in large venues such as stadiums, arenas, and other public spaces. Venues must identify the type of access needed for different areas of the premises. This includes setting up access control points, such as gates, doors, and turnstiles, as well as managing blacklists (e.g. hooligans). An effective access control system is essential for large venues to ensure the safety and security of all visitors, staff, and property.

Access Control

Our solution:

Sentinel Access Control recognizes in an instant whether guests or employees have the necessary access authorization. Access control for major sporting and entertainment events has been greatly simplified- venue staff will be onboarded much quicker and people on blacklists will effectively be denied access.   

Biometric Ticketing

Our solution:

Biometric ticketing offers a convenient and efficient solution for attendees to gain entry to the event. With the use of Sentinel Access Control, attendees can register their biometric data in advance, which can then be used to quickly verify their eligibility to enter the venue and grant them access to the event.
