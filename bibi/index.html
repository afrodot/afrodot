About Me

Hello, I’m Boitshepo Bibi Giyose, I serve in a technical leadership and management capacity in the design and implementation of priority development programmes, especially food and nutrition security and social protection programmes and initiatives at regional and international levels. I improve food systems food security, nutrition, livelihoods, health and overall well-being of populations especially the underprivileged and marginalized.
